What Nilea helps you see

What it does

  • Collects signals from your real interactions — especially DMs
  • Helps you group and recognise subscriber behaviour patterns
  • Shows where something may deserve your attention
  • Supports your decisions: monitor, set boundaries, ignore, or escalate

What it doesn't do

  • No automatic punishments or accusations
  • No fear-driven alerts
  • No surveillance or enforcement
  • Nilea never decides for you
